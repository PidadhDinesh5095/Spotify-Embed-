Seamless streaming with my music player

Spotify Embed player

It is a music player that offers features like song search, different categories, and a user-friendly interface for seamless music exploration. Users can easily find and play their favorite tracks while enjoying an organized and smooth listening experience. This project was developed using Spotify's APIs, integrating its playback and data services to fetch songs, albums, and playlists.
